I'm an American singer-songwriter, guitarist, and record producer. Born in Bridgeport, Connecticut, I attended Berklee College of Music in Boston, but dropped out and moved to Atlanta in 1997 to pursue music fulltime.

I've recorded over ten albums, including a number of live albums. I've played with big names such as B. B. King and Eric Clapton.
